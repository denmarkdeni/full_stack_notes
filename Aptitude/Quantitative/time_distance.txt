 2. A cyclist covers a distance of 800 meter in 4 minutes 20 seconds. What is the speed in km/hr of the cyclist?

a) 10 km/hr
b) 12 km/hr
c) 16 km/hr

a. 2 : 1
b. 1 : 2
c. 2 : 2

a. 2.45 p.m.
b. 2.30 p.m.
c. 1.35 p.m.
d. 1.15 p.m.
