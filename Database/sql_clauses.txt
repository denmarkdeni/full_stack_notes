9. WHERE vs HAVING

WHERE
ğŸ‘‰ Filters individual rows BEFORE grouping
ğŸ‘‰ Cannot use aggregate functions

HAVING
ğŸ‘‰ Filters grouped results AFTER aggregation
ğŸ‘‰ Can use COUNT, SUM, AVG, etc.

>>>> Examples

SELECT 
    customer_id, 
    SUM(amount) AS total_spent
FROM orders
WHERE amount > 100              -- Step 1: Remove small orders
GROUP BY customer_id            -- Step 2: Group remaining rows
HAVING SUM(amount) > 400        -- Step 3: Keep only big spenders
ORDER BY total_spent DESC;
