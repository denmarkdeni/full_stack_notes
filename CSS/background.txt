Background 
    background 
        we can apply many properties in background
        linear , radial gradient
    background-color
        red , blue , green 
    background-image 
        url("bg.jpg");
    background-repeat 
        Options: repeat, repeat-x, repeat-y, no-repeat
    background-size
        auto(default), 
        cover(sizing the element to cover the background),
        contain(full image in background , may have spaces), 
        or width height (100px , 50px)
    background-position
        left, right, center , left top, 50% 50%, 100px 200px
    background-attachment
        scroll ( background also scroll), 
        fixed (background fixed when page scroll)
    background-clip 
        border-box, padding-box, content-box , text
